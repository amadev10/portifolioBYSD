More About Me

I am a passionate Electrical Engineer with strong expertise in industrial systems, automation, and energy solutions. My academic background includes a degree in Electrical Engineering from the Catholic University of Mozambique. Over the years, I’ve enhanced my skills through certifications in PLC programming, medium voltage operations, electrical safety, and more.

Professionally, I’ve held key roles in major projects, including:

  • Senior Technician at FURA Gems – overseeing electrical systems in mining operations.

  • Manager at JV Rousant & Elemech – leading energy infrastructure projects.

I’m skilled in designing and maintaining electrical systems, troubleshooting complex issues, and ensuring energy efficiency and safety. I'm constantly seeking to improve and innovate in every task I take on.

I thrive in dynamic environments, enjoy leading technical teams, and value continuous learning. Let’s build powerful, sustainable, and smart electrical systems together.
